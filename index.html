Divinatio offers daily sky readings for each of the twelve signs, drawn from historical astrological traditions spanning Greek, Arabic, Persian and Vedic sources. Each reading is generated fresh by an automaton trained on centuries of such texts, using live planet coordinates retrieved from the Astronomy API.

This art is thousands of years old. It has been dismissed, revived, distorted, commercialised, and dismissed again. What survives underneath all of that is something genuinely useful: a structured way of asking what is happening, inside and out, and whether those two things might be related. That question never went out of date.

Divinatio carries no advertising, no engagement algorithm, and no interest in flattery. An independent project, built to be quietly useful — and occasionally beautiful.
