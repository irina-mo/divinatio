About

Divinatio.art

The sky has been read for thousands of years as a vocabulary for understanding who we are and what we are moving through.

The tradition behind these readings is thousands of years old. It has been dismissed, revived, distorted, commercialised, and dismissed again. What survives underneath all of that is something genuinely useful: a structured way of asking what is happening, inside and out, and whether those two things might be related. That question never went out of date.

Divinatio offers daily sky readings for each of the twelve signs, drawn from historical astrological texts spanning Greek, Arabic, Persian and Vedic traditions. Every reading is generated fresh each day using the actual positions of the planets at the time of writing. Planet coordinates are sourced in real time from precise ephemeris data — the exact longitude of the Sun, Moon, Mercury, Venus, Mars, Jupiter, Saturn, Uranus, Neptune and Pluto for any given moment. From that geometry, the reading is composed.

What does a waning Moon in Virgo mean for a Scorpio in early May, with Pluto slow in Aquarius? The reading is an attempt to answer that honestly, in the spirit of ancient observation applied to an ordinary day.

An automaton trained on centuries of astrological and philosophical texts reads the sky each morning and divines. Planet positions are retrieved in real time from the Astronomy API — the exact longitude of each body at the moment of writing.

Divinatio carries no advertising, no engagement algorithm, and no interest in flattery. An independent project, built to be quietly useful — and occasionally beautiful.
